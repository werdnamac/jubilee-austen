A little bit about me

I've made my home base in Providence, Rhode Island, with my small growing family. My journey into tech started with a degree in journalism. As I started sharing my writing online, I was fascinated with how easily I could get my voice out there. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific needs.

That curiousity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned to build my own website, I realized I'd found something that gave me the freedom and versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and attenion to detail to write clean, elegant code.

Hand-Coded HTML

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and easy to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date with current best practices.
